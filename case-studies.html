Trump-Cohen Case

About the Case

This case involves former President Donald Trump and his attorney Michael Cohen. At the core of the controversy are issues related to attorney-client privilege, ethical responsibilities of legal professionals, and campaign finance violations.

Some Facts of the Case Included:
  • When under investigation, Michael Cohen broke attorney client privellege
  • Cohen gave Trump advice that was not legal
  • Michael Cohen at the end of the case was disbarred, unable to practie law again
  • President Trump was not charged with anything in this case
  • Cohen served time in prison, but was released a couple years later, due to Covid concerns

    • Why wasn't he pardoned?

    Michael Cohen was not pardoned from his time by the current active President at the time, likely due to President Trump feeling betrayed by his own personal lawyer for breaking attorney client privilege with him, exposing their private conversations.
